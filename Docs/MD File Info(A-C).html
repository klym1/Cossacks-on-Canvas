* BORNPOINTS
$ BORNPOINTS n n n
[] /
& BUILDINGS
+ CONCENTRATOR
^
= where the unit will appear if it comes out of the building. Born Points are points you want to declare as accessable as these are the points a unit will follow if it is created and exits the building ( or vice versa, enters or STORMS the building). The first number is the amount of x & y positions in pairs, then followed by those coordinates -x,y (in 16x8 blocks) relative to the top left hand corner of the picture area.

*  COSTPERCENT
$ costpercent n
[] /
& UNITS & BUILDINGS
+ /
^ COSTPERCENT 150
= This will increase the price of the next unit by x % (150 in example). Note that all gsc game's suffer from something like a max price inflation cap. Depending on the initial cost and price inflation, the price will suddenly not increase anymore, this goes for all buildings and units. More experience players who have played epic game's know this, but you gotta test this for yourself to avoid unwanted results. You can ofcourse increase this to a ridiculous amount of lets say 99999999999999 or a negative value for creative use of this string.
